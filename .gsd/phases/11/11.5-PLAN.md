# Phase 11.5: Flutter Architecture for Arc & Coach

## Objective
Display the new Narrative arcs intuitively in the Flutter App visually and incorporate the Hybrid Coach Service.

## Tasks
1. **App Lifecycle Binding**
   - Utilizing `WidgetsBindingObserver`, hook into app resumes to fire `/metrics/session_start` telemetry for the backend.

2. **GDI Coach UX**
   - Based on the `GDI` score, display subtle UI "Ambient" indicators in `home_tab.dart` rather than intrusive modals (following Commitment Gradient Descent).

3. **Narrative Arc Labeling**
   - Update `goal_roadmap_screen.dart` to stylize milestones into their designated movie-arc positions (e.g. Act 1: Setup, Climax, etc.).
